About me

Who I am, what I enjoy, and what I can bring.

I’m Dalvin, a Multimedia & Creative Technologies graduate who enjoys turning ideas into practical, visually considered projects across design, digital fabrication, coding, and prototyping.

Who I am

I like work that sits between making and thinking. My approach is hands-on, but I still care about structure, clarity, and the final presentation. I want my projects to look considered, but also feel useful and grounded.

I’m most motivated by projects where I can combine visual design, technical problem solving, and a practical build process into something that actually works in the real world.

Interests

What I enjoy working on

  • 3D design and physical making
  • Product development and prototyping
  • Programming and interactive systems
  • Digital fabrication and craft-based workflows

Studies

What I studied

I graduated in Multimedia & Creative Technologies, where I built a base in design thinking, digital tools, fabrication processes, and the technical side of creative work.

That background taught me how to move from an idea to a tested concept, then into a more polished and presentable result.

Experience

What I’ve done

My experience comes from combining college assignments with self-driven projects. I’ve worked on physical builds, digital concepts, CAD exercises, and projects that mix electronics, programming, and fabrication.

I’m comfortable moving between concept work, iteration, testing, and the final presentation stage.

How I can help

I can help shape an idea into a clear outcome, whether that means designing a product, building a prototype, preparing files for fabrication, or combining digital and physical elements into a single project.
